SDV AmsterDance presents...
Arabian Nights Ballroom Gala
10th of February, 2017
Rhone Events: Humberweg 5, Amsterdam. 20:00 - 0:30
Join us for our grand AmsterDance 5-year Anniversary Ballroom Gala at a very special location! Experience the wondrous world of flying carpets and belly dancers!
Come and dance a waltz or a rumba to the music of 1001 nights on our sultan-sized dance floor. Submerge yourself into the flair of the Middle East during two magical dance performances in an atmosphere of colourful lights and candles.
Everyone is welcome at our Arabian nights themed ballroom Gala. Currently, 0 may still receive the Sultan-package!

Performances

At two different times during the evening there will be magical dance performances, both worthy of the great Padishah. Enjoy the show of an enchanted belly dancer and a renowned ballroom dancing couple. Shortly we will announce the performers on our Facebook page


Dress code & costume competition

We are having a themed celebration, so our guests are expected to dress accordingly. We hope to see belly dancers, Arabic nomads and Jinn. Show us your creativity! We will hold a dress competition in which every member of the celebration will cast their vote for the best costume. The winner will be rewarded with a special surprise!

Should you not have the option to wear a specialized costume then your dress code is black tie. The sultan will be displeased to see guests in casual clothing.


Sultan ticket (VIP)

Only the highest viziers may receive a very special sultan treatment. The highest viziers may seat in a separated section of the hall with the best view on the performances, enjoy a selection of exquisite eastern snacks and tea, and are granted access to a VIP-only cushion corner. Please indicate BEFORE Wednesday 8th February 23:59 if you are a high vizier so that we can offer you a worthy treatment!


Snacks and drinks

Dancing for a long time makes hungry. The Padishah will generously provide small snacks throughout the evening. Refreshments (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) can be acquired at the Sultan's bar.


Can't ballroom dance yet?

Fancy about the grand gala, but afraid that your dancing skills are not good enough? Not a problem! AmsterDance is offering a workshop in ballroom dancing on the 1st of February for those who are interested. For details please visit the Facebook page of the workshop .


More questions?

If you could not find the answer to your question, please contact us via email at gala.amsterdance@gmail.com or contact us on Facebook.
